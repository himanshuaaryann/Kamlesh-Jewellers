✦ Who We Are ✦

Our Legacy

Since 2002, ZANZAR JEWELLERS PRIVATE LIMITED has been Ahmedabad's most trusted name in gold, diamond, and silver jewellery. Located at near, 22 -23,Dhananjay Tower ,100Ft Road, Shyamal Cross Rd, opposite Vraj complex, Satellite, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380015, we combine 24 years of artisanal craftsmanship with a simple promise — no middlemen, no markups, just honest luxury.

We offer same-day delivery, no making charges, and a collection built for every occasion — from everyday elegance to grand bridal moments. Every piece that leaves our store carries with it two decades of passion, precision, and pride.
